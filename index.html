Secure, Contain, Protect

Welcome

To The SCP Files

Welcome to the SCP Foundation, a secretive and global organization dedicated to securing, containing, and protecting anomalies that defy the laws of nature. These anomalies, known as SCPs (Special Containment Procedures), range from dangerous creatures and objects to entire realities that could pose a threat to humanity. The Foundation operates in secrecy, conducting research to understand and neutralize these anomalies while safeguarding the general public from their potentially catastrophic effects. Its primary mission is to ensure that these mysterious and often dangerous entities remain contained, hidden from the world, and prevent their misuse or destruction.

SCP - 001

The "Gate Guardian" is an ancient, towering entity guarding a massive, seemingly indestructible gate. Its purpose and true nature remain unclear, but it is capable of immense destructive power, preventing anyone from approaching the gate without dire consequences.

SCP - 002

Known as "The Living Room," SCP-002 is a large, spherical, organic mass that contains an interior resembling a furnished living room. It is capable of expanding and consuming anything that comes into contact with it, adding it to its environment.

SCP - 003

"Biological Motherboard" is a complex, sentient entity composed of both organic and mechanical components, which has the ability to alter and control electronic systems. It exhibits aggressive tendencies when provoked or manipulated.

SCP - 004

"The 12 Rusty Keys and the Door" involves twelve keys that unlock a specific door, each with a different reality-altering property. These doors lead to different anomalous locations, often with dangerous consequences for those who enter.

SCP - 005

SCP-005 is a set of keys that can unlock any door, regardless of material or security measures, including doors that are normally indestructible. The keys remain unbroken despite their apparent age and wear.

SCP - 006

"Fountain of Youth" is a spring that produces a liquid with life-extending properties, effectively granting immortality to those who drink from it. However, prolonged exposure to the water causes extreme mutations in living organisms.
